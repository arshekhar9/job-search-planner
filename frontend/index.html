💌 Cold Outreach Ideas

✉️ Cover Letter Guide

  1. Hook: Open with the specific problem the team is solving and why it excites you.
  2. Fit: Map 2–3 of your achievements directly to the job description using metrics.
  3. Vision: Share a short, sharp insight about what you'd prioritise in the first 90 days.
  4. Close: End with a confident, specific ask — a 20-minute call, not a vague "hope to hear from you."

🤝 Outreach Preparation Guide

  1. Research first: Read the person's last 5 LinkedIn posts or published work before reaching out.
  2. Be specific: Reference exactly what the team shipped or announced recently.
  3. Give before you ask: Share a relevant insight, article, or observation before asking for anything.
  4. One clear ask: A 15-minute call > coffee > informational interview. Pick one and commit.
  5. Follow up once: Wait 5–7 days. One follow-up is professional; more is spam.

📐 Product Design

  • CIRCLES framework for product design
  • Define user, goals, pain points first
  • Prioritise with effort vs. impact matrix
  • Always tie features back to metrics

📊 Analytical / Metrics

  • Define the north star metric first
  • Practice metric degradation diagnoses
  • Know AARRR and HEART frameworks
  • Prepare A/B test scenarios

🧠 Strategy

  • Market sizing with bottom-up approach
  • Competitor analysis: strengths, moats
  • Build vs. buy vs. partner decisions
  • Porter's Five Forces for market entry

💬 Behavioural

  • Prepare 6 STAR stories (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
  • Cover: leadership, conflict, failure, ambiguity
  • Tailor stories to each company's values
  • Practice out loud — not just in your head

🤖 AI / Technical (for AI roles)

  • Understand LLM capabilities and limitations
  • Know AI safety basics (for Anthropic/OpenAI)
  • Be ready to discuss responsible AI tradeoffs
  • Study company model cards and research blogs
